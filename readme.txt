== Description ==

A blogger's new best friend, Riddle makes it easy to quickly create beautiful and highly shareable quizzes, tests, lists, polls, and more. With just a few clicks, rapidly embed any Riddle on your Wordpress blog - with no coding required - and each can be easily shared on Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter and more!

= Features =

- Stunning quizzes, personality tests, top10 lists, polls, and more.
- Use any of our 1000’s of licensed images or upload your own?
- Go beyond pictures - use GIFs, YouTube videos, Soundcloud, Vines and more for added ‘pop’!
- Built-in virality: each Riddle can quickly be shared on Facebook and other social networks - and all these resulting new users come back to your site.
- No coding required - just copy and paste a shortcode into a new post.
- The kicker? It’s 100% free!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Download the zip file
2. Extract the zip file on your local machine
3. Copy the extracted folder riddle to your wp plugin (/wp-content/plugins/) directory via your ftp program of choice.
4. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
5. Find any Riddle you would like to embed on Riddle.com, and click on the Wordpress logo in the top right corner. Place the resulting Wordpress short codes in the text view of your post: (example) [postriddle data_game="{post url automatically generated by riddle.com}" data-width="100%" data-height="auto" data-recommendations="true" data-share="false" data-comments="false" data-info="false"]
6. [Click here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KovuUimghpI) to a one minute tutorial on creating your own Riddle.

(Helpful tip: if you would like the entire Riddle to display on your post so users don't have to scroll down to complete the Riddle, you can change the data-height to data-height="100%".)

Any questions not covered here? Drop us a line at hello@riddle.com - we'd love to hear from you!
   

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I create my own content? =

Something missing on your site? 
You can't find a specific Riddle perfect for your favorite subject? 
Perhaps you need a Riddle to test people's Gaelic language skills? Or a top 10 list about the most underrated designers at New York's fashion week? 
No worries, we've got you covered. You can create your own Riddles as long as you stick to our Riddle guidelines (essentially no porn, hate or spam riddles). 
Login and click the big + icon on the top right. You can create polls, hot or not comparisons, quizzes and top 10 lists. 
They each come with a handy little "how to" help guide to get you started. 
The riddles you create will look awesome on your own web site or blog. So go ahead use Riddle to create interactive fun for your readers, friends and family.

= Will this plugin slow down my website? =

No, we're bloggers ourselves - and hate anything that makes sites lag.

= What do I need to use this plugin? =

We require at least Wordpress version 3.0.1 and a connection to our servers at riddle.com to keep your system always up to date with the newest tests.

= Which PHP version is required? =

Currently we require at least 5.3. If you are using older versions please contact us at hello@riddle.com and we'd be happy to help you out.
