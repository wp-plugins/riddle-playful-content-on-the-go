Riddle has the latest playful pop culture quizzes and tests online. 

== Description ==

If it's hot and trending in pop culture, you can be sure that Riddle will have something fun (and funny) for you to do and share. 
It might be tests that make you "hm..." or quizzes that make you laugh out loud. To riddle is fun - to riddle with friends? Awesome. 
What can you find on Riddle? Anything and everything.

= Features =

- Tests: Have you taken our 'Who's your rock star partner in crime?' test?
- Quizzes: Can you score better than your friends on the music of the last millennium?
- Top 10 lists: From the inspiring to the ridiculous: Top 10 best opening lines in novels or the top 10 funniest chat up lines. 
- Hot or Not?: A knock out competition for the prettiest woman, the sexiest man or maybe just deciding the perfect eye candy for you right now!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Download the zip file
2. Extract the zip file on your local machine
3. copy the extracted folder riddle to your wp plugin (/wp-content/plugins/) directory via your ftp program of choice
4. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
5. Place the wordpress short codes in your posts (find the posts within metabox or on riddle.com)
   [postriddle data_game="{post url from riddle.com}" data_width="100%" data_height="auto"]
   

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I create my own content =

Something missing on your site? 
You can't find a specific riddle to tickle your fancy or cover your favorite subject? 
Perhaps you need a riddle to test people's Gaelic language skills? Or a top 10 list about the most underrated designers at New York’s fashion week? 
No worries, we got you covered. You can create your own riddles as long as you stick to our riddle guidelines (essentially no porn, hate or spam riddles). 
Login and click the big + icon on the top right. You can create polls, hot or not comparisons, quizzes and top 10 lists. 
They each come with a handy little “how to” help guide to get you started. 
The riddles you create will look awesome on your own web site or blog. So go ahead use Riddle to create interactive fun for your readers, friends and family.

= Does this plugin has negative effects to the performance of my website? =

No, the content of riddle is loaded absolutely asynchronously.

= Which dependencies do this plugin require? =

We require at least Wordpress version 3.0.1 and a connection to our servers at riddle.com to keep your system always up to date
with the newest tests.
